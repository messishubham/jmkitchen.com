01 The Kitchen

A Bungalow Reimagined for Modern Jaipur

Set inside a restored Jaipur bungalow, Jaipur Modern Kitchen carries the quiet character of the original home into a contemporary café and kitchen.

The space preserves a sense of proportion, natural light, material honesty, and handcrafted detail — a setting where food, craft, design, and conversation move naturally together.

Less like a standard restaurant, more like a modern Jaipur home — opened for fresh food, slow meals, and thoughtful gatherings.
